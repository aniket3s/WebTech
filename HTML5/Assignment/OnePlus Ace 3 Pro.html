OnePlus Ace 3 Pro

  • Display: 6.78-inch LTPO AMOLED with 120Hz refresh rate and HDR10+ support.
  • Processor: Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 chipset for seamless multitasking.
  • Battery: Massive 6100mAh battery with 100W fast charging.
  • Camera: Triple rear cameras (50MP primary, 8MP ultrawide, 2MP macro) and a 16MP front camera.
  • Storage: Options up to 1TB with 24GB RAM for heavy users.
  • Build: Sleek design with Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2 protection.
  • Connectivity: 5G-ready, Wi-Fi 6/7, and Bluetooth 5.3.
  • Durability: IP65-rated for dust and water resistance.
  • Audio: Stereo speakers with Dolby Vision support.
  • Software: Runs on Android 14 with ColorOS 14.
